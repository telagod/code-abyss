# Variant Selection And Performance / 风格选型与性能

## 1. Choose By Product Fit

- neubrutalism: bold, graphic, cheap to render
- claymorphism: soft and friendly
- glassmorphism: layered and modern
- liquid-glass: premium and delicate

## 2. Performance Questions

- is blur affordable on target devices
- do shadows stack too heavily
- is motion essential or decorative

## 3. Review Questions

- does the chosen style fit brand and user expectation
- what rendering cost does it add
- what is the fallback for weaker devices
